Breakfast Burrito

Description

A classic breakfast burrito featuring bacon as the main protein. This burrito has the classic fixin's of potatoes, eggs and cheese. A special honey sriracha sauce will be added to give this dish a flavorful kick.

Ingredients

  • Large Tortilla
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 3 Slices of Bacon
  • 1/2 Cup Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Hashbrown
  • Quarter of Avocado
  • 3 tbsp Sriracha
  • 3 tbsp Honey

Steps

  1. Bake slices of bacon at 350 for 12 minutes.
  2. Saute hashbrown with oil for 5 minutes or until crispy.
  3. saute eggs with oil and cheese, for about 2 minutes.
  4. slice avocado.
  5. combine sriracha and honey.
  6. Assemble all ingredients.
