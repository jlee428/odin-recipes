Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Description

These crispy and juicy air fryers are one of the easiest yet tastiest recipes to prepare. This recipe is one of the cheapest and most convenient meals, involving zero knifework and no preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs Chicken Thighs (Skin On)
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 1 sprig Rosemary

Steps

  1. Season chicken thighs with all dried seasonings
  2. Preheat air fryer at 400 for 5 minutes
  3. Air fry chicken thighs for 25 minutes, flip halfway
